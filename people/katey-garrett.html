Katey M. Garrett

Attorney

Katey Garrett practices in the areas of criminal defense and habeas corpus law. She is admitted to the practice of law in the State of Connecticut.

Katey joined the firm in March, 2019. Before joining the firm, she worked as an attorney at a large national insurance defense law firm, and she worked as a paralegal at a large insurance company. During law school, she served as a Note Editor of Western New England Law Review, she worked as a paralegal at the same large insurance company, and she worked as a paralegal at a small law firm.

Katey graduated from Western New England School of Law in 2017, where she received a Juris Doctor degree, and from Bay Path College in 2013, where she received a Bachelor of Arts degree in legal studies.
