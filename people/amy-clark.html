Amy S. Clark

Paralegal

Amy Clark helps Damon Kirschbaum and Amy Kirschbaum manage all aspects of the firm. Amy C. is the first point of contact for all administrative and financial issues.

Amy C. joined the firm on January 6, 2014, which makes her the firm's longest-tenured employee. Before joining the firm, she worked as a legal assistant at two other law firms, and she worked as a caseworker for an agency that provides behavior management, family support, and crisis intervention to high-risk children in an intensive residential setting.

Amy C. graduated from Northeastern University in 2011, where she received a Bachelor of Science degree in criminal justice, with a minor in sociology, and from Suffield Academy in 2006.
