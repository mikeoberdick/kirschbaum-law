Jessica M. Walker

Attorney

Jessica Walker practices in the areas of criminal defense and habeas corpus law. She is admitted to the practice of law in the State of Connecticut, the United States District Court for the District of Connecticut, and the United States Supreme Court.

Jessica joined the firm in August, 2018. Before joining the firm, she worked as an attorney for more than 10 years at four other law firms, where she practiced in the areas of criminal defense and civil litigation.

Jessica graduated from the University of Connecticut School of Law in 2006, where she received a Juris Doctor degree, with honors, and from the University of Connecticut in 1999, where she received a Bachelor of Liberal Arts degree, summa cum laude.
